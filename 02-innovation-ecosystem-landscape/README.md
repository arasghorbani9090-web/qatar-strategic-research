## Why this matters

Qatar's research-funding architecture restructured significantly under QRDI's 2022 reorganization. The active funding pillars for 2026 — Academic Research Program, National Research Program, Research Talent Program, Strategic Calls (AIQAT, Agentic AI, PPM), and Innovation Pathways (QSTP, QFTH, QDB/Invest Qatar) — operate under distinct eligibility, funding-amount, and delivery-format rules.

Most major academic calls require **Lead Principal Investigator institutional affiliation**, a structural constraint that shapes who can compete for research funding in Qatar. The landscape document maps this and identifies where the structural opening for non-affiliated and SME research delivery actually exists (largely: procurement-system tenders, semi-government supplier registration, and innovation-program pathways).

## How to use this folder

- **Government agencies** evaluating funding-mechanism gaps — `ECOSYSTEM_LANDSCAPE.md` is a meta-analysis of who funds what
- **Research institutes and universities** mapping competitive positioning — the channel inventory is structured by category
- **Innovation programs** benchmarking their position in Qatar's portfolio — comparative table of QRDI vs QSTP vs QFTH vs QDB program design
- **Consulting firms** scoping Qatar engagements — `REGULATORY_FRAMEWORK.md` documents entity, taxation, and procurement pathways
